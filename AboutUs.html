Who are we?



Wayside Veterinarian Clinic is Veterinarian Clinic that specializes in taking care of all animal types. Ranging from everyday house animals like cats and dogs, to more exotic animals such as bearded dragons and parrots. We have been in business since 2000, and our customers keep coming back for our quality care and competive pricing. If you would like to schedule and appointment with us, please contact 281-779-5168 to schedule an appointment. We are open all days between 9AM to 6PM.
